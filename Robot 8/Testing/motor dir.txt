R UP + L UP = Reverse / Shoot Cata

R DOWN + L DOWN = Forward / Wind Cata ---

R UP + L DOWN = Reverse Intake
R DOWN + L UP = Forward Intake


E UP = Reverse Intake
E DOWN = Forward Intake

F UP = Forward Intake / Backroller No Torque
F DOWN = Forward Backroller / Intake No Torque unless supported by other motors
